About

Bernardo Nunes | UX/UI Designer

My passions revolve around illustration, photography, and traveling. I also enjoy the art of aquascaping, creating beautiful and balanced underwater landscapes. I live in Belas, Portugal with my wife and work remotely as a UX/UI Designer. I started my career as a salesman at Vodafone to pay for my studies, but soon realized that my true calling was in graphic design. My background in visual arts, combined with my continuous learning in experience design and user insights, led me to pursue a career in UX/UI design. For the past 2 years, I have been a key member of the engineering team at OutSystems. I collaborate with product management to identify improvements and new additions that will positively impact our users. I also create videos to showcase our team's vision for complex user journeys, and work with the product design team to explore solutions and create hi-fi prototypes. Additionally, I assist the UX research team with testing and gathering insights, and am dedicated to improving our team's work by participating in our central practice of UX/UI. I also contribute to the design system by using our libraries and providing feedback on the design of new components and guidelines. My ultimate goal is to create user-friendly experiences that enhance people's lives and bring value to the projects I work on. I am constantly seeking new opportunities to grow and develop my skills in UX/UI design, and bring my passion and expertise to every project I work on.
